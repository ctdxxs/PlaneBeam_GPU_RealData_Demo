Welcome to minifus Functional Ultrasound Imaging Platform

minifus is a high-performance, open ultrasound platform designed for cutting-edge scientific research. We are committed to providing scientists with powerful tools to explore the infinite possibilities of ultrasound imaging.

This website will introduce you to our device overview, 3D structure, and software platform.

Platform Features

  • Real-time Functional Imaging: Supports real-time brain functional imaging without saving raw data
  • High-precision Detection: Capable of detecting micro-blood flow changes with spatiotemporal resolution of 100 μm / 100 ms
  • Open Platform: Flexible MATLAB-based control and data analysis environment, also supports C/C++ software development

Technical Advantages

The minifus platform adopts ultrafast ultrasound imaging technology, abandoning traditional line-by-line scanning methods. Through single-shot emission covering the entire imaging area, combined with advanced algorithms, it achieves high-frame-rate, high-resolution real-time functional imaging.
